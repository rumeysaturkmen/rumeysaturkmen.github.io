What I work on

Hi! I am Rumeysa. I am PhD candidate at Concordia University. My work explores how people interact with virtual and augmented reality environments. I design and evaluate interaction techniques using gaze, hands, and voice input, with a focus on making 3D selection and manipulation more accurate, efficient, and usable.
