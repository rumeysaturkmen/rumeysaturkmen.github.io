About Me

Hi, I'm Rumeysa (she/her)! I am pursuing my PhD degree in Computer Science at Concordia University, where I study how people interact with virtual and augmented reality environments.

My research is motivated by a simple question: how can we design interaction techniques that make immersive environments more accessible, efficient, and usable? To explore this, I design and evaluate interaction techniques with gaze, hand tracking, or voice input, particularly for selection and manipulation tasks. I have also worked on 3D sketching and 3D pointing studies.
